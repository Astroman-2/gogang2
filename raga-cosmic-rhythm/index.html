The Living Grammar of Sound

Rāga is not a scale — it is a complete universe of melodic movement. The word derives from Sanskrit rañj — "to colour, to tinge with emotion." Unlike Western modes, each rāga carries strict laws of ascent (āroha), descent (avaroha), characteristic phrases (pakad), and emotional essence (rasa).

The 72 Melakarta System

The Carnatic tradition organizes all heptatonic scales into 72 parent scales (melakartas), from which hundreds of derived rāgas emerge — a combinatorial cosmology of sound.
